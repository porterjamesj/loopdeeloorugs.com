his collection of loopdeeloo rug hooking patterns

          springs from a lifelong love of antique textiles. Katherine Porter, inspired by a variety of historical styles, and informed by an ongoing, twenty-year career as a

surface pattern designer, strives to create rug patterns

that are traditional, yet clean and contemporary.

While the designs are meant to be hooked with wide- to medium-cut wool, a finer approach would certainly work as well. The rug patterns are offered on your choice of high-quality linen or monk’s cloth and are drawn by hand with acid-free pens on the straight grain of the fabric.
